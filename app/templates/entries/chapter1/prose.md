This is a **chapter** - a numbered entry in your manuscript.

The frontmatter above (between `---` markers) controls how your chapter appears
when exported. The `title` field overrides the name shown in the navigator.
Uncomment `subtitle` or `epigraph` to add them to your chapter opening.

Chapters are numbered in the order they appear. Reorganize them with `K` and `J`
in the navigator.

You can group chapters into **sections** (like "Part 1", "Part 2") but there are
none by default. Add a section from the manuscript root in the navigator.

Each entry (chapter or page) has:
- `prose.md` - your prose (this file)
- `notes.md` - private notes that won't be exported
- `snippets.json` - text snippets for this entry (coming soon)
- `comments.json` - inline comments (coming soon)
